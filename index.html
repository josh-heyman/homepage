Projects

Filament Machine

A-Level Design and Technolgy

The project aim was to develop a machine capable of recycling plastic bottles and turning them into filament to be used by 3D printers. The design had to take into consideration the sustainability of materials used, the safety of the operator, and had to be easy and simple in its operation.

iMac Teardown

Creating a display showing the inner workings of iMac

Given an old iMac, i was tasked with creating an exploded view of the device to help students understand how many parts interact with each other to create a product. It also provides an insight inot the complex circuitry required for computers to function.
